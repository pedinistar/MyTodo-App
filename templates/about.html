About MyTodo

MyTodo is designed to simplify the way you manage your tasks. Our mission is to help you become more organized and productive.

Our Mission

MyTodo is designed to help you stay organized and manage your tasks with ease. Whether you're juggling multiple projects or simply need a way to keep track of personal to-dos, this application offers flexibility and simplicity to get things done efficiently.

Features

    Effortlessly Add, Update, and Delete tasks.
    Organize your tasks by priority and deadline.
    Track your task creation dates for better time management.
    Fully responsive design with Bootstrap for mobile and desktop use.

Technologies Used

    Flask - Backend framework for rapid development
    SQLite - Lightweight database for fast storage
    HTML, CSS, Bootstrap - Modern frontend technologies
    SQLAlchemy - ORM for easy database management
